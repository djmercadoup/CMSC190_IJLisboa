Global human modification across terrestrial lands. Black represents areas with no mapped modification (HM = 0), dark blue in low modification (0.00 < HM ≤ 0.10), purple in moderate modification (0.10 < HM ≤ 0.40), and yellow in high modification (0.40 < HM ≤ 1.00). Note that low modified lands are areas with low mapped human influence and is not necessarily equivalent to the extent of native vegetation
